This page provides access to the official HypurrFi brand assets, including logos, icons, and other visual elements for public use.

## Available Assets

The repository includes the following categories of assets:

* **HypurrFi** - Official HypurrFi logos, wordmarks, and lockups
* **HypurrFi Cats** - HypurrFi cat mascots and related imagery
* **Last Network** - Last Network brand assets
* **USDXL** - USDXL token and branding assets

## Usage Guidelines

When using these brand assets, please:

1. Do not modify the logos or alter their colors
2. Maintain appropriate clear space around the logos
3. Do not use the logos in a way that suggests partnership or endorsement without permission
4. Do not use the logos on backgrounds that impair visibility

For questions about brand usage or to request additional formats, please contact the HypurrFi team at [socials@hypurr.fi](mailto:socials@hypurr.fi).
