# Hyperliquid Resources

Resources and documentation for Hyperliquid ecosystem

Hyperliquid is the fastest growing perpetuals and spot DEX in crypto with a clear trajectory to become a hub for onchain global finance. The sidecar EVM enables liquidity bootstrapping, derivative lending, and niche financial use cases with 1-second transport between the massive Hyperliquid liquidity engine.
