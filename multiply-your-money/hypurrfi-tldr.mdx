## Trade

DEX trading terminal with full composability between trading venues, access to capital from lenders, and earning yield on your profits.

Builder code-enabled trading terminal for advanced trading strategies and easy access to capital.

## Earn

Deposit to lending markets and vaults for traders to access yield and liquidity. Pooled and Euler markets and vaults for better yield on your assets.

Levered spot positions looping on yield-bearing LSTs and stablecoins. Traders borrow, you earn.

## Spend

The credit Card spends directly from your onchain collateral position. Spend without selling and tap to pay at 100M+ merchants worldwide.

Each tap routes through HypurrFi's real-time credit infrastructure, pulling from your HypurrFi Markets positions in either Borrow Mode or Spend Mode. No top-ups or off-ramps are required.

## Grow

Grow your project's RWA and stablecoin adoption with boosted DEX liquidity, active lending markets, Hyperliquid Spot listing, Hyperliquid Perps listing with primarily protocol-owned liquidity.

Asset issuers partner with HypurrFi to kickstart liquidity in the Hyperliquid ecosystem.
