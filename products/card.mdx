The Swype Credit Card lets you spend directly against your on-chain positions without selling assets. Each tap routes through HypurrFi's real-time credit infrastructure, pulling from your HypurrFi Markets positions in either Borrow Mode or Spend Mode. No top-ups or off-ramps are required; everything executes on-chain at the moment of payment.

## Overview

The Swype Card is a credit card that allows users to spend from lending positions without selling crypto.

Please refer to the [Swype FAQ documentation](https://docs.brahma.fi/faqs/swype.fun-or-faqs) for information and troubleshooting.

## Eligibility

## Interest and Repayment

Interest rates are variable at whatever rate for the asset in the HypurrFi Market. These rates change with utilization of available assets in the market.

Users can refinance into a different borrow asset at any time, all interest rates are variable and are listed prominently on the HypurrFi Market App.

There is no scheduled payment time. Interest accrues every second, but it's designed to smoothly compound to the annual APY you see. In other words, you don't get charged 10% every second, but if you had this rate over the course of the year, you would end up paying 10% interest in total.

Repay spending with any supported asset or refinance your borrow to a different rate within HypurrFi Markets.

## How It Works

### 1. Deposit & Collateralize

Deposit HYPE or other supported assets to the pooled markets on HypurrFi.

### 2. Spend & Earn

Use your card for everyday purchases while your collateral continues earning yield. Earn HypurrFi Points on each spend.

### 3. Repay & Optimize

The spend is taken as a borrow action on HypurrFi, resulting in a USDT0 borrowing position. Repay your spending with any supported assets or refinance your borrow to a better rate.

### 4. Earn & Spend

Switch to Spend Mode to deposit USDT0 and earn the lending rate on HypurrFi, then spend down the balance with each Card tap.

## Spend Mode

Deposit USDT0 into Spend Mode to earn yield, then spend down the balance with each transaction as if the card were a prepaid card.

Plus the 0.5% workflow execution fee above.
