HypurrFi Earn Vault depositors allocate to curated DeFi and trading strategies for passive yield generation. HypurrFi Earn Vaults are designed for passive yield seekers who want exposure to Hyperliquid DeFi without managing individual lending or borrowing positions.

HypurrFi Earn Vaults are made with the Euler Finance codebase, and follow the same change parameters as HypurrFi Markets, that is, no changes to the underlying code, but independent risk and deployment parameters from the Euler team.

<Note>
  The term "vaults" in the Euler stack is used both as a fundamental lend-borrow primitive, and as a deposit strategy to earn yield. Earn Vaults are the specific type of curated vault that directs funds to particular markets and financial strategies and returns yield to depositors.
</Note>

HypurrFi Earn Vaults act as yield aggregators by deploying deposits across curated strategies. Risk curators actively manage these vaults by rebalancing strategies, maintaining liquidity, and assessing risk profiles.

Users benefit from aggregated yield generation while delegating the underlying complexity to professional managers.

Key features include a simple deposit interface (metamorpho), diversified yield strategies, and non-custodial safety—risk curators can rebalance allocations but cannot remove user funds. Continuous rebalancing across DeFi strategies aims to maintain efficiency and optimize returns.

Earn Vaults can be deployed by any curator and displayed on their own front end. These vaults use HypurrFi Markets. However, HypurrFi is extremely selective about the Earn Vaults displayed on the HypurrFi front end, selecting only those curators that have passed due diligence review demonstrating conservative risk tolerance, excellent monitoring and rebalancing practices, and a track record of prudent strategy management.

## How to Deposit

1. Go to [Earn Vaults](https://app.hypurr.fi/earn) and browse available vaults
2. Review each vault's strategy, curator, APY, and deposited asset
3. Click into a vault and enter the amount you want to deposit
4. Approve the token if this is your first time
5. Confirm the deposit transaction

Your deposit is managed by the vault curator. Yield accrues to your position automatically.

## How to Withdraw

1. Go to the vault you deposited into
2. Click **Withdraw** and enter the amount
3. Confirm the transaction

Withdrawals are subject to available liquidity in the vault. If the vault has deployed most of its capital, you may need to wait for rebalancing or redemptions.

## Curators

Earn Vaults allow curators to deploy their own vaults for user deposits to earn via the curator's strategy.

Initially, only select partners are enabled as curators on the HypurrFi front-end. Independent deployments of HypurrFi Earn Vaults run strategies via HypurrFi Markets, but HypurrFi is imposing a strict gradual release for curators on the front-end application.

To inquire about joining the HypurrFi front-end curator cohort, please contact the HypurrFi team directly.
