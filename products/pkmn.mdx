# PKMN

Index of high value Pokemon collectibles as a spot, perpetuals, and lending market asset

PKMN is the umbrella product for an index of high value Pokemon collectibles as a spot, perpetuals, and lending market asset.

Like gold with various grades and qualities, a gold index takes into account the value of a wide variety of gold prices and markets. PKMN is a baseline value index for high value cards, not tracking any single card, but rather the base set value from which markets and single card valuations can correspondingly reprice.

The outcome is an index that is a tradeable vehicle for real-world TCG value at a benchmark rate, creating more sophisticated portfolio strategies in tandem with the highly modular and capital efficient Euler Market stack and Hyperliquid perpetuals infrastrucutre.

Details about the foundation structure, deposit vaults, curator and custodian arrangements are in development.
