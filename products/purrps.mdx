Purrps is the HypurrFi DEX using Hyperliquid builder codes with integrated lending and spend.

Purrps takes advantage of Hyperliquid execution and liquidity, HypurrFi Vaults, and credit card spend against your collateral position to create seamless capital flows between lending positions and the Hyperliquid liquidity engine.
